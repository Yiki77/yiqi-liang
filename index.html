Hello, I'm Yiqi

...sometimes I go by my English name, Yiki!

I am a second-year M. Phil. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) under supervision of Prof. Mingming FAN and Prof. Yuyu LUO. I am a member of Accessible & Pervasive User EXperience (APEX) Group.

I am interested in Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. I like to design and use different tools of AI and generative models to tackle different vision tasks. More recently, I am learning and doing research on Human-Computer Interaction(HCI). My research currently centers around the intersection of Computer Graphics and Human-Computer Interaction(HCI), exploring themes around Computer Vision, Human-AI Collaboration with Generative AI and Assistive Technology.

Please reach out if you think we can collaborate or share ideas :)
